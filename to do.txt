PerlString To-Do List
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following items must be done before PerlString will be ready for 
release:

- Regular Expression support.
- split()
- crypt()
- pack() / unpack()
- Optimization, especially in the CPP version.
- Wide character or Unicode support. This will probably only happen for the STL
  version.
